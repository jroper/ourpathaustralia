Australian chapter

We are the Australian chapter of OurPath, an international LBGT+ affirming organisation that has been serving partners of LGBT+ people since 1986.

  • Get Support

Get support

OurPath offers a range of support options, including personal support. We are, at our core, a peer-to-peer support network. All of our Support Contacts have traveled their own path with a current or former partner who is (or is believed to be) LGBT+. We get it.

Contacting us through OurPath's international support request page will allow you to be linked up with Australian Support Contacts and Australian forums.
